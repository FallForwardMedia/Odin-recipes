Spaghetti Recipe

Instructions!

This is a recipe displaying how to make one of my favorite dishes, spaghetti! We will start out by boiling the water for our pasta and turning the stove on medium heat.

Following this, we will wait for the water to reach a boil, and add some salt to the water as well.

Once the water reaches a boil, we add our noodles to the pot and let them sit in the boiling water for 10 minutes.

Once this is done, we add the pasta sauce to the noodles and season it to create the final product.

Ingredients!

    Spaghetti Noodles
    Pasta sauce (homemade or store bought)
    Seasonings such as italian seasoning, salt, and oregano
    6 cups of water
    Preferably shredded parmesean cheese
